FEATURE SPOTLIGHT — AUTOMATIC PRICE UPDATES FOR OFFLINE SUPPLIERS

Here's the feature to put in your pocket: automatic, invoice-based price updates for offline suppliers.

The problem it solves: for offline or private suppliers, prices drift out of date, buyers see stale numbers, and that erodes trust in the whole platform. We heard exactly that friction in a customer workflow review last week, where pricing inaccuracies were undermining user confidence.

Who it's for: essentially any customer with offline or private suppliers — which is most portfolios.

How to demo it: show the opt-in toggle on the buyer-supplier connection, upload an invoice, and let the price update on its own. Then show the price history with the new updated-date field, and point out that any manual price the buyer set is respected.

The competitive angle: BirchStreet announced an AI "Smart AP" product back in February, so buyers are hearing a lot of AP-automation noise. This is a concrete, unglamorous, trust-building win — keeping pricing accurate without manual babysitting.

Discovery questions: How many offline suppliers are you ordering from? How do you keep their pricing current today? And who on your team is catching stale prices before they cause a problem?

The likely objection — "we set prices manually" — has a clean answer: manual prices are respected, and the updated-date field gives you a clear history of every change.
